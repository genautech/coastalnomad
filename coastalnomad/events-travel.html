💡 Insider Travel Tips

Expert advice from Rodrigo to make your visit seamless

📱

Get a Local SIM

Purchase a prepaid SIM at the airport. TIM and Vivo offer great data plans for ~$10-15/week.

💳

Currency & Cards

Brazilian Real (BRL). Credit cards widely accepted. Use Wise or Revolut for best exchange rates.

🏨

Accommodation

Stay near properties you want to visit. Rodrigo can recommend hotels with flexible check-in.

🍽️

Best Time to Visit

Year-round destination! Dec-Mar for summer beach vibes, Apr-Nov for tech events and cooler weather.

🗣️

Language

Portuguese is the official language. English widely spoken in tech hubs and tourist areas.

⏰

Time Zone

BRT (UTC-3). Plan your video calls accordingly. Most nomads adjust within 2-3 days.

🔌

Power & WiFi

127V/220V outlets (Type N). All properties have fiber internet 100-500 Mbps.

🏥

Health & Safety

No required vaccinations. Private healthcare excellent. Travel insurance recommended.
